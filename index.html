About us

WE ARE TASTY

Uzbek Corner London is your Uzbek restaurant located in London, a stone's throw from Kensington Gardens. At Uzbek Corner London, you will be able to find an incredible Uzbek cuisine able to bring together the most delicious flavors from Central Asia. Important : Come inside the market to find us !

At Uzbek Corner London, enjoy many homemade preparations made by our chef and his inimitable talent with love, care and attention. Try our delicious Borsch and its unique taste as starter, our superb Plov (rice, lamb, carrots, garlic, sultanas) as main dish or let yourself be tempted by our no less tasty Somsa (pastry with lamb, onions, herbs). At Uzbek Corner London, we strive to offer you a true culinary experience in a warm, pleasant and refined atmosphere, so that you can enjoy the preparations you have chosen in the best possible way. Ask for a delivery, or live the experience on the spot or to take away. But do not forget : Pre-order in order to avoid waiting time.
